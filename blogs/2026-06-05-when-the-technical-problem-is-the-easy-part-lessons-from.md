Getty Images ingests several terabytes of telemetry data daily with 30% projected yearly growth, and used Cribl to cut ingestion volume by nearly half while keeping costs flat despite business expansion. The real challenge was not the technology but organizational buy-in, with intake forms and stakeholder pain-point mapping proving more critical than any technical configuration.
