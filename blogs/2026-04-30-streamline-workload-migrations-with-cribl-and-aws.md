Workload migrations are rarely as simple as flipping a switch. For most IT and security teams, moving to the cloud means managing a massive surge in telemetry data while trying to avoid visibility gaps and security risks. When you are in the middle of a migration, the last thing you need is a blind spot in your logs, metrics, traces, and events.
