Modern IT and security teams face a flood of telemetry data. Logs, metrics, and traces each describe part of system behavior, but using them separately creates blind spots, slows investigations, and increases costs. Combining logging, metrics, traces and context such as network and service topology into one monitoring and observability strategy helps teams detect issues faster, find root causes efficiently, and control costs without losing data quality.
