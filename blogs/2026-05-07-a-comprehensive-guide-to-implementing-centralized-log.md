Centralized log management collects logs from servers, applications, firewalls, databases, cloud services, and devices into a single searchable repository to improve visibility, speed up troubleshooting, and support compliance. For IT operations, security, and engineering teams dealing with growing telemetry volumes, it turns scattered data silos into a managed, shared service that enables faster incident response and better decision-making.
