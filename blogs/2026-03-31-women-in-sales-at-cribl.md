In a tech landscape where sales floors can still look a little too much like a “before” photo, the women on Cribl’s sales team are busy flipping the script—not waiting for permission. This spotlight on six women in frontline sales roles *happens* to fall in Women’s History Month, but let’s be clear: their impact isn’t limited to one month on the calendar. They’re building pipeline, challenging assumptions, and proving—daily—that great sales talent doesn’t come with a default setting.
