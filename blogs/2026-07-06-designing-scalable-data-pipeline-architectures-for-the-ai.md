Modern enterprises generate telemetry and operational data at large scale, yet many struggle to turn that data into usable insights. The difference between organizations that succeed and those that fail often lies in how well their data pipeline architecture scales. A well-designed pipeline moves data efficiently, absorbs traffic spikes, adapts to schema changes, and ensures the right data reaches the right destination at the right time.
