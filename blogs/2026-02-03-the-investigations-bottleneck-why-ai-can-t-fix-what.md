AI promises total automation for security investigations, but the reality is that teams are hitting a wall: investigations remain agonizingly slow. The failure isn't the AI, it’s the data feeding the machine. AI can’t accelerate a workflow when telemetry is unstructured or scattered across a dozen disconnected tools.
