Telemetry is only useful if you can trust it. Logs, events, and metrics need to arrive on time, in the right shape, and at the right volume—or downstream tools, investigations, and automations quickly fall apart. Cribl Insights gives IT and Security teams a centralized view into what’s happening across their entire Cribl environment.
