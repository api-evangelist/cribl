Cribl Packs have long been the gold standard for packaging and sharing complex telemetry pipelines. But as deployments grow from single-digit to multi-region Worker Groups, even "portable" configurations can turn into copy‑paste debt. Group Variables for Packs and Enhanced Pack Cloning move Packs from simple portability to true environment-aware automation.
