Data pipelines aren’t a back-office concern anymore. They’re the production line for every metric, dashboard, alert, and model you depend on. A reliable data pipeline is an automated system that moves, transforms, and delivers data from source systems to destinations such as warehouses, lakes, or analytics tools, enabling teams to act on accurate, timely information without babysitting brittle jobs or chasing missing fields.
