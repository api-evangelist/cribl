AI assistants like Claude Cowork are often framed as chat interfaces: You ask a question, get an answer, and move on. But in practice, we’re seeing AI assistants move well beyond simple chat experiences. They call tools and connectors, read and modify files, and take actions on behalf of your users, and that’s exactly where things get interesting for security, compliance, and operations teams.
