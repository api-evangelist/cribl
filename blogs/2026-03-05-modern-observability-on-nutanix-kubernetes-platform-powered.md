Running Cribl Edge on Kubernetes clusters managed by the Nutanix Kubernetes Platform gives you a single, consistent way to observe what’s really happening in your platform. Instead of stitching together agents, sidecars, and custom exporters, Cribl Edge sits close to the workloads on each node, collecting metrics, logs, and events and turning them into usable, structured signals you can route anywhere. This post isn’t a reference architecture you must follow.
