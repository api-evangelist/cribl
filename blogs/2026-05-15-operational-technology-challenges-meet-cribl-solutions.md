Operational Technology (OT) environments are some of the most challenging places to deploy modern observability and security tooling. You’re dealing with highly segmented networks, strict safety and compliance requirements (NERC CIP), and systems that were never designed to talk directly to the internet. At the same time, those environments are full of high‑value telemetry: logs, metrics, and events that can help you improve reliability, detect threats, and reduce downtime, if you can get to them in a safe and cost effective manner.
